ABOUT

Deep Sea Studios is an arts collective and film production company based in Riga, Latvia. Founded by young artists and producers, we operate as platform where new talents work alongside experienced professionals to create new and enriching artistic experiences.

Though we are a young company, our film productions have already won several awards and have been featured in world-class industry events such as the Goteborg Film Festival and Krakow Film Festival.

In addition to our own work, we are open for collaboration with artists from Europe and beyond. If you are looking for co-production opportunities in the Baltics, feel free to get in touch with us.
